What is Success?

Success is a term misinterpreted by many people. Success is real success. It is a person or thing that has achieved a “good result by acceptable means” and been successful, or the fact of achieving something that you have been intending to do or garner by acceptable means. Underscore the phrase ‘good result by acceptable means’, the sole criterion by which success is measured. Yes, only a good result is what we call success. A good result or success denotes the utilisation of the right way. For a good result to be called good, it ‘must’ be generated from the execution of the right way.
